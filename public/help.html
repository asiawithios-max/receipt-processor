🔐 Logging In

1
Go to the app URLYou will be redirected to the login page automatically if you are not signed in.
2
Enter your full name and passwordUse the exact name your account was created with. First login uses the temp password IOS2025! — you will be prompted to set your own password immediately.
3
You are routed to your home pageSales reps go to the rep home. Customer service goes to the CS view. Super admins go to the dashboard.
Sessions expire after 1 hour. A timer in the top right shows time remaining. You will be returned to the login page when it expires — nothing is lost.

📝 Creating a Receipt

1
Tap the + buttonFrom your rep home screen tap the gold + button in the bottom right corner to start a new receipt.
2
Your name is pre-filledYour account name fills in automatically as the Sales Rep. You can edit it if needed for a co-sale.
3
Set the sale dateDefaults to today. Change it if the sale happened on a different day.
4
Enter customer informationName, address, city/state/zip, and email.
5
Select sale company and productsChoose IOS or Limitless. Select all products that apply. Some products have a notes field for terms or specifics.
6
Enter payment detailsAmount (numbers only, no $ sign), payment method, card last 4 and expiration if credit or debit. Set payment # of # for payment plans.
7
Review and submitStep 4 shows a full summary. Review carefully then tap Submit and Generate PDF.
8
Copy the signing linkThe link auto-copies to your clipboard on the success screen. Text or email it to the customer immediately.
9
Mark as SentAfter sending the link tap Mark Sent on the submission to record the timestamp permanently.

✍️ How the Customer Signs

1
Customer opens the linkWorks on any phone, tablet, or computer. No app or account needed.
2
They review the receiptAll products, amount, payment info, and full terms and conditions.
3
They draw their signatureUsing their finger or mouse in the signature box.
4
They tap I Agree and SignThe signature is stamped onto the PDF with the date and a unique electronic signature ID.
5
They download their copyA Download Signed Receipt button appears so they can save their copy.

📊 Status Guide

Unsent Receipt created, signing link not yet sent to customer

Sent Link marked as sent, customer has not opened it yet

Viewed Customer opened the link but has not signed yet

Signed Customer signed — PDF is complete and locked

✅ CRM Confirmed — shown as a green badge when admin or CS has saved the signed receipt to the company CRM

👥 Account Types

Sales Rep

Submit receipts, see your own submissions, edit unsigned receipts, archive, get signing links, download PDFs.

Customer Service

Read-only view of all submissions. Copy signing links, download PDFs, mark CRM Confirmed. Cannot submit or edit.

Super Admin

Full access. Create and manage all accounts, see all submissions, confirm CRM, export data, delete submissions.

📄 PDF Types

⬇ Signed PDF

Current version of the receipt. Includes customer signature if signed, blank signature line if not.

📄 Unsigned PDF

Always the blank template as originally submitted. No signature ever. Use this when you need a clean original copy.

🔗 Signing Link

  • Generated automatically when the receipt is submitted
  • Auto-copies to clipboard on the success screen
  • Always available in the Signing tab on the submission detail
  • The same link works every time — resend it anytime
  • Never expires
  • Shows Already Signed if the customer opens it after signing

👁 Link Tracking

Every time a customer opens the signing link it is automatically tracked. In the Signing tab of any submission you can see:

  • When the link was first opened
  • When it was most recently viewed
  • How many times it has been opened

Status automatically updates from Sent to Viewed when the customer opens the link.

✏️ Edit Receipt

You can edit a receipt after submission as long as it has NOT been signed. To edit:

  • Open the submission detail
  • Tap the Edit button
  • Type EDIT in the prompt and tap OK
  • Change the fields you need
  • Tap Save Changes — PDF updates automatically
Locked fields: customer name, products, company, sale date. Once signed, editing is disabled entirely.

📤 Mark Sent

After sending the signing link to the customer tap Mark Sent on the submission. This records a permanent timestamp. Cannot be changed once set.

🗄 Archive

Archiving hides a submission from your main list but keeps it permanently in the admin dashboard. To archive tap the Archive button in the submission detail. To see archived submissions scroll to the bottom of your list and tap the Archived section. You can unarchive from there.

You can also bulk archive — tap Select, check multiple submissions, then tap Archive Selected.

✅ CRM Confirmed

After saving a signed receipt to the company CRM, admin or CS taps CRM Confirmed on the submission. A green CRM badge appears on the submission letting the rep know their sale has been fully processed.

⧉ Duplicate Form

Creates a copy of a receipt with all fields pre-filled. Use when a customer pays with two cards, a sale involves both IOS and Limitless, or a customer makes a second purchase with similar products.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. What do I do? ▼
Contact your admin to reset your password. They can reset it to the temp password from the dashboard. You will be prompted to set a new one on your next login.
I made a mistake in the receipt. Can I fix it? ▼
Yes — if the receipt has not been signed yet you can edit it yourself. Open the submission detail, tap Edit, type EDIT to confirm, make your changes, and save. The PDF updates automatically. If it has already been signed it is locked — contact admin.
The customer needs the signing link again. How do I find it? ▼
Find the submission in your list, tap it to open the detail, go to the Signing tab. The link is always there. Tap Copy Link and send it again.
What does Viewed mean? ▼
Viewed means the customer has opened the signing link at least once but has not signed yet. It updates automatically the moment they open the link. You can see exactly when they last viewed it in the Signing tab.
What is CRM Confirmed? ▼
CRM Confirmed means admin or customer service has saved the signed receipt to the company CRM. It shows as a green badge on your submission so you know your sale has been fully processed without having to ask anyone.
What if the sale is for both IOS and Limitless? ▼
Submit the receipt for one company first. On the success screen tap Duplicate Form. Change the company on the copy and adjust any other fields. Submit the duplicate. You will have two receipts and two signing links — send both to the customer.
What if a customer pays with two different cards? ▼
Submit the first receipt with the first card and amount. Duplicate the form. Update the amount, card last 4, expiration, and payment number on the copy — for example Payment 2 of 2. Each card gets its own receipt and signing link.
My session expired. Did I lose anything? ▼
No. All submitted receipts are saved permanently in the database the moment you submit. Signing links, signatures, and all data are safe. Just log in again and everything will be there.
Can I use this on my phone? ▼
Yes — the app works on any phone, tablet, or computer. In Safari on iPhone tap Share then Add to Home Screen to add it as an app icon for quick access.
What is the difference between Signed PDF and Unsigned PDF? ▼
Signed PDF is the current version — includes the customer signature if they have signed. Unsigned PDF is always the blank template as originally submitted with no signature. Use Unsigned PDF if you need the original receipt without any signature.
